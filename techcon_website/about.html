History

TechCon started in 1989 as a small student-led tech meetup in a university basement.
By the mid-1990s, it grew into an annual convention called “TechCon,” attracting hobbyists,
startups, and local companies. In the 2000s, it became a regional hub for technology innovation,
introducing tracks on web development, cybersecurity, and mobile computing. By the 2010s, TechCon went global,
featuring international speakers, live-streamed keynotes, and discussions on AI, robotics, and digital ethics.
Today, it is a major forum connecting innovators, entrepreneurs, and tech enthusiasts worldwide
